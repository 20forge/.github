───────────────────────────────[ LORE ]───────────────────────────────

  Nov 16, 2025. One commit: 'Init: the forge begins to burn.' No plan
  B, no turning back. Two thousand commits later, the fire still
  burns. So does the scope.

  Deep in the repo lie the ruins of ember-ui, the prototype that came
  before the Smithy. Nobody cleared the rubble. Old fires temper new
  steel.

  The forgemaster is Danilo, a Game Master who got tired of running
  sessions with eleven tabs open. He decided the fix was to build all
  eleven himself. Classic GM logic.

────────────────────[ HOUSE RULE: MADE BY PEOPLE ]────────────────────

  In tabletop RPG, AI is a touchy subject, and we get why. So
  instead of leaving you to guess, here it is plainly:

  The idea is always human. AI is a tool, never the author. In a
  forge, the tool shapes nothing on its own. The hand on the
  hammer decides.

  Everything creative comes from people: art, words, the way each
  tool talks to you. AI is an extra pair of hands and an editor
  hunting for a comma out of place. It never decides what 20Forge
  has to say. Technology serves the table; it never takes its
  place.

  "This is my manifesto."
  Danilo, RPG Master and Master of 20Forge

  Read it in full: https://20forge.gg/manifesto/ai
